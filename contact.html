Contact Me


Thank you for showing interest in my work! If you're inquiring about a commission, please include as much detail as possible such as what you'd like embroidered, how large you want it to be, if you'd like anything special added, if it's for a gift or you need it by a specific deadline. I will try to accomodate deadlines as best I can! I will let you know an estimated time frame for your order before you pay. Deposits are required before I begin any commission work. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask!
